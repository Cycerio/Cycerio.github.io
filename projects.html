PDF Unduplicator

Worked as the lead contributor to the PDF Unduplicator, a desktop application capable of scanning through PDF files of any size, removing duplicate pages, and identifying those that were potentially outdated. This was necessary as numerous government files contained redundant or outdated data. This information served no use and only bogged down servers which slowed progress immensely. These PDFs were initially being cleaned by hand, but the PDF Unduplicator reduced the time needed to remove the unnecessary pages from about an hour to under a minute.

Data Retrieval Tool

Worked as the lead contributor to the company's Data Retrieval Tool, a desktop application built using Pyqt5 and Pandas, capable of quickly visualizing, sorting, and merging large amounts of data with ease. When I arrived the company had a very rudimentary tool in use. I was tasked with extending the tools current features, fleshing out those not yet in place, and then subsequently optimizing the tool to improve its runtime performance.
