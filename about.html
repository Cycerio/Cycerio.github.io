A Bit About Me

Hello! My name is Nick Campbell and thanks for visiting my website! So, where to begin? Well for starters why did I decide to become a programmer? That’s easy, I love puzzles. When I was younger I got so much joy out of brain-teasers and logic-puzzles and being a Software Engineer allowed me to continue getting that same joy. While not exactly the same, many of the stages of development mimic the mental hurdles I go through whenever I approach a puzzle and I think that’s fantastic. On a related note, this is why I enjoy game development so much. As it presents similar obstacles while also feeding into many other outlets of creativity. While I prefer staying on the programming side of things, I’ve begun to dabble in sound design and art and it has honestly been a lot of fun. On the topic of music, I’ve been playing piano for a few years now. I’m no professional, but it has been a blast to pick up. I was a band-kid throughout middle and high school, and while I no longer play saxophone, it feels good to continue putting everything I learned to use. I also practice Shotokan Karate, and am currently a Blue Belt in the JKA, but unfortunately am currently looking for a new dojo to train at. My other hobbies include reading, I’ve been chipping away at The Stormlight Archive for some time now and Gaming, with my four favorite game series being Halo, Dark Souls, Kingdom Heights, and Pokemon. Furthermore, I plan to upload some of my past projects to this website eventually, but there is still some refining I would like to do beforehand.
